OpenOLAT Resources


This help course serves as a portal for all available OpenOLAT online resources. Below you find a list of all official resources. Feel free to add any resources you deem helpful, by means of editing this course.

Resources are available in English only.

  • OpenOLAT Manuals  Here you can find a list of user manuals, installation manuals, technical documentation, demo courses, articles and more.
  • OpenOLAT Mailing list  Mail feed of ongoing discussion of the OpenOLAT mailing list / google group. You can subscribe to the feed.
  • Community Server  Want to check out the OpenOLAT community? Register or login here.
  • Twitter Feed  Interested in what others have to say about OpenOLAT? Listen in here.
